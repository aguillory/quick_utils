Important Feeding Rules

Do NOT Overfeed: Bottle babies always act hungry due to their instinct to suckle. Stick to the calculated amounts.
  • Milk Type: Feed Whole Cow's Milk or Whole Goat's Milk.
  • Baking Soda: Add a pinch of baking soda to the first bottle of the day to aid digestion.
  • Temperature: Warm milk to approx 101°F - 103°F.
  • Assessment: After feeding the suggested amount, feel the baby's tummy. It should feel firm, not rounded out or sunken in. Adjust feeding amounts accordingly.
